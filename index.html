How to Play

Your goal is to survive as many waves as possible and move your way up the leaderboard by defending replit's headquarters from bugs, malicious users and repls, and more.

Place towers by opening up the tower menu at the bottom of the screen and upgrade them to provide better defenses. Some special one-time attacks can be used as well.
